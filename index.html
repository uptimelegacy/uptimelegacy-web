How UptimeLegacy helps you avoid downtime

Whether you are facing an unexpected breakdown or planning for upcoming obsolescence, UptimeLegacy gives you a single point of contact to source the right parts, at the right time and condition, to protect your uptime.

Emergency breakdown sourcing

When a line is down, we move fast. Share your part numbers or upload your list, and our team will search our network for available stock, compatible alternatives and express shipping options.

Stock replenishment & maintenance planning

Build the right spare parts strategy for your installed base. We help you identify critical spares, quote stock packages and manage replenishment for scheduled maintenance.

Obsolescence & lifecycle management

Plan ahead for end-of-life components. We support last-time buy decisions, identify form-fit-function replacements and propose migration paths where possible.

Exchange, repair & retrofit options

Extend the life of your automation assets. Depending on your part and budget, we can offer exchange units, tested refurbished options or repair routes via trusted partners.
